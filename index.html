Sustainability
We strive to lighten the furniture footprint on the planet and take new responsibility in the process with a fully local approach to design and manufacturing. All of our furniture is built with FSC Certified Domestic hardwoods, and other sustainably-sourced materials. 90% of which are grown and harvested within 70 miles of our production facility.
